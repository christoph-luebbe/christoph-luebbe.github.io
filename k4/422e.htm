4.2.2 Trigonometric Functions

A further group of fundamental functions for all natural sciences which you already know from school are the trigonometric functions. They play a central role in all periodic processes, whether it is in space or in time, for example during the oscillation of a pendulum,  for the description of light or sound waves, and even for the vibration of a string.
