9.3.1 Vector Sum

While for the points of three-dimensional Euclidean space there was no reason to think of any arithmetic operations, it makes sense from the physical point of view to ask for arithmetic operations involving vectors. For example several  displacements can be implemented  one after the other: After we shifted a mass point e.g. from the point in accordance with the vector to the point , we can shift it further afterwards from according to the shift prescripton of the vector to the point.
