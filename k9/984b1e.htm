Further Literature



The German PDF version of this course (without animations and the solutions for the exercises) may be printed from the internet as a script (e.g. to be taken to the beach). An English PDF version is in preparation. In addition there is a German book edition of the course available in book stores with most figures in black and white which clearly can not contain the animations and the function plotter, but which includes solutions of the exercises in an appendix.

Naturally the content of the relevant literature goes far beyond what we have discussed here. In order to avoid anticipation of things which may be in your future lectures we deliberately do not recommend individual textbooks. However, you will find below a list in alphabetical order suggesting a variety of newer and older textbooks waiting for you in libraries. This list is presented without comment to avoid chaneling your enthusiasm and to make it easier for you to maintain an overview when immersing yourselves in books during the upcoming time.

Before buying a book you should have looked into it several times "successfully".
