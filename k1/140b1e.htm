While these prefixes of the SI system are internationally fixed, this is by no means so with our familiar number words . The  Anglo-American and also French expression "billion" in the above table means the German "Milliarde" = 109 and is different from the German Billion = 1012. "The origin of our sun system 4.6 billion years ago..." must be translated as "die Entstehung unseres Sonnensystems vor 4.6 Milliarden Jahren...". Similar things apply to the Anglo-American "trillion"  = 1012, while the German "Trillion" = 1018.
