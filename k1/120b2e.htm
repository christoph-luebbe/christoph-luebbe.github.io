Denotations:

It is an accepted thing in international physics for long time past to abbreviate as many of the physical quantities as possible by the first letter of the corresponding English word, e.g. s(pace), t(ime), m(ass), v(elocity), a(cceleration), F(orce), E(nergy), p(ressure), R(esistance), C(apacity), V(oltage), T(emperature), etc..

Of course there are some exceptions from this rule: e.g. momentum p, angular momentum l, electric current I or potential V.
