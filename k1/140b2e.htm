Other unit names:

Special names are also still used for some metric units: You know perhaps 102 m2 as are,  104 m2 as hectare,  10-3 m3 as litre, 102 kg as quintal und 103 kg as ton.

Do you also know 105 Pa as bar, 10-28 m2 = bn as barn,  10-5 N = dyne,  10-7 J = erg,  10-15 m = fm  under the name of  Fermi,  10-10 m = 1Å  after Ångström or 10-8 Wb under the name of Maxwell?
