Differently as in differentiation there is no procedure of integration which automatically after a sufficient amount of work leads to the goal. This is the reason why integration is an art while differentiation can be called atechnique.

Nevertheless, there are a lot of rules and tricks which follow from the characteristics of the integral and can make our lives considerably easier. Therefore we want to turn out attention now to those: the simplest one is the  linear decomposition.
