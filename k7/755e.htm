Nowadays the information collected in these tables is also incorporated in programs such as  MATHEMATICA or MAPLE . These find the primitive functions, if they exist, even in difficult cases. You should not be afraid of using these tables and programs in your daily work. The use of these programs though requires in general some knowledge of the different integration techniques and tricks, because otherwise the given comments and constraints on the regions of validity can not be taken into account appropriately and correctly. This is the reason why we have studied these problems here.
